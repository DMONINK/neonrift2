HOW TO PLAY

DESKTOP CONTROLS

AD / ◄►Move Left / Right
SPACE / W / ▲Jump — tap again for Double Jump
SHIFT / XDash — burst of speed (direction-aware)
S / ▼Dodge Slide — brief invincibility window
ESCPause / Resume

MOBILE

Use the joystick to move. Tap JUMP to jump — double-tap for a double jump in mid-air. DASH for a quick speed burst in the direction you're facing. DODGE slides under obstacles with a cooldown timer shown in the HUD.

POWER-UPS

🧲Magnet — Pulls energy shards toward you
⏳Slow-Mo — Everything slows to 35% speed
💨Dash Boost — Longer, faster dash distance
⚡Invincibility — Full immunity; damages enemies on contact
✨2× Score — All points doubled
🔥Rapid Fire — Instant-kill enemies on contact

ENEMIES & LEVELS

8 enemy types grow in difficulty as you run. Every level has a Boss Guardian — deplete its HP bar to advance. Reach Level 5 to face The Collapse. Build your combo by passing enemies and collecting shards — it decays over time! Spend earned energy in Upgrades to power up between runs.
